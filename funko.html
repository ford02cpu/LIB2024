What is a Funko Pop!?

About Funko Inc.

Funko Inc. is a company that produces licensed pop culture collectibles, known in particular for its Funko Pop! line of vinyl figurines with ‘chibi’ dimensions: oversized heads, small bodies and black circular eyes. They claim to hold “the world’s largest selection of pop culture licenses.” 1

The company’s headquarters are located in Everett, Washington. The company was founded by Mike Becker in 1998 and first obtained the licensing to produce Big Boy merchandise (a restaurant mascot). In 2005, the company was sold and expanded its licensing agreements and product offerings. 2

Currently, Funko has licensing agreements with numerous entertainment companies, including Lucasfilm, Disney, Paramount Pictures, DC Comics, and many more. Through these agreements, fans can purchase Funko collectibles for characters as varied as Captain Kirk to Pennywise the clown, anime characters to video game characters.

Funko offers a range of products, all based on the stylized appearance of their Pop! figures:

  • Funko Pop! figures (these come in a variety of sizes)
  • Pop! Deluxe and Pop! Town figures, which are figures accompanied by additional props such as thrones or buildings
  • Keychains
  • Bobbleheads
  • Mugs
  • Plush toys
  • Clothing and purses
