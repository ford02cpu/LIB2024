Funko Pop! Figurines vs. Traditional Figurines

One better than the other?

Consider the following images:

Can you tell which are Funko Pop! figures and which are more traditional figures? The differences are pretty stark! Let's list the differences in a table!

Differences
Funko Pop! figurines Traditional figurines
'Chibi' dimensions Realistic body dimensions
Large, square head and small body Proportional body and head
Circular, black button eyes Realistic eyes
Limited variety in poses More variety in poses, action scenes

So... Is one type of figurine better than the other?

Conclusion: It is all a matter of personal preference!
